Why should I create a personalised autism infographic?

You are unique

As are all autistic people. There is no one explanation that fits all.

You're worth to be understood

We are convinced that the right explanation can lead to more acceptance.

Explaining isn't allways easy

By creating an infographic you will be prepared for those situations where you can't find the right words or don't have the energy to speak.
